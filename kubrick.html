Kubrick's influence is everlasting on cinema

Kubrick has produced such rich works that we can put him onto a high pedestal and revere him as a master of the cinema and its methodologies. Here in my blog we will go over all the major movies that have been directed by Kubrick and also look at some of his lesser known movies that came at the beginning of his career. So without waiting let us start. And why don't we start at that one that has us going wild with us amazement and puzzle even after half a century of its release!

2001: A Space Odyssey

Kubrick for me is known for his enigmatic slow form of storytelling that can be famously realised in his 1975 movie Barry Lyndon. The story captivates at the level of the soul with its keen exploration of the lead character and his rise and fall. We come to despise him at a point in the story but by then we have already felt pity on him which also happens at the end of it. But at the end it is real cinema. It makes us think about ourselves and think critically.
