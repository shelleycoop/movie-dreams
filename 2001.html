50 Years is a Big Deal. What can change in 50 years?

Not the reputation of this movie.

The way it astounded and puzzled its audiences more than 50 years ago, it does so even now. And what it did to push the limits of storytelling? Maybe not explicitly, maybe. But it did challenged its viewers to engage with the story in a way which is far more challenging and involved. It was a much more of a reaction than simply catharsis which was a common trait of the movies those days. But cinema was changing even then and that is a good sign because times change as well.
